CredSetu - Trust Score & Group Health Logic

This document breaks down the exact points system and logic behind the Trust Scores and Group Health Scores in the CredSetu platform.

================================================================================
1. INDIVIDUAL MEMBER TRUST SCORE
================================================================================
The Member Trust Score evaluates an individual's financial reliability.

Scale: 
- Minimum Score: 0
- Maximum Score: 900
- Starting (Base) Score: 100

How Points are Added:
- Verified Saving Deposits: +2 points per deposit
- On-time Loan Repayment: +5 points per repayment
- Successfully Completed Loan: +20 points (awarded when loan status is 'completed')
- Meeting Attendance: +1 point per meeting attended

How Points are Deducted (Penalties):
- Late Loan Repayment: -10 points per late repayment
- Defaulted Loan: -20 points (applied when loan status is 'defaulted')
- Missed Meetings (Consecutive): -5 points (applied when a member misses 3 meetings in a row)

Updates: The score recalculates immediately whenever a new transaction, attendance record, or loan status change happens.


================================================================================
2. GROUP TRUST SCORE (GROUP HEALTH SCORE)
================================================================================
The Group Health Score measures the collective financial stability of an entire Self Help Group (SHG).

Scale:
- Minimum Score: 0%
- Maximum Score: 100%

The score uses 4 weighted formulas to calculate a total percentage:

Component A: Average Member Trust Score (Weight: 40%)
- Logic: Takes the individual trust scores of all members, averages them, and converts it to a 100-point scale.
- Formula: (Group Average Trust Score / 9) * 0.40

Component B: On-Time Loan Repayment Rate (Weight: 30%)
- Logic: Calculates the percentage of on-time loan repayments compared to all repayments made by the group.
- Formula: [ (Total Repayments - Late Repayments) / Total Repayments ] * 100 * 0.30

Component C: Fund Growth Goal (Weight: 20%)
- Logic: Rewards groups that maintain a healthy active fund balance. The current target is ₹10,000. Reaching this target grants the full 20%.
- Formula: [ Total SHG Fund / 10,000 ] * 100 * 0.20 (Maxes out at 20%)

Component D: Pending Loan Penalty (Weight: -10% Deduction)
- Logic: Acts as a penalty for having too many loans stuck in a "Pending" status relative to total loans.
- Formula: [ Total Pending Loans / Total Loans ] * 100 * 0.10

Final Group Score = (Component A) + (Component B) + (Component C) - (Component D)

The final score is mathematically rounded and strictly clamped between 0 and 100. It refreshes automatically anytime an individual member's data in the group changes.


================================================================================
3. RISK ENGINE & AUTOMATED ALERTS
================================================================================
A background system automatically fires alerts based on score thresholds to notify admins and banks:

High Severity (Red Alerts):
- Group Critical Status: Group Health Score drops below 50.
- Member Critical Status: A Member's Trust Score drops below 60.

Medium Severity (Yellow Alerts):
- Repayment Risk: A member accumulates 2 or more late repayments.
- Engagement Risk: A member misses 3 consecutive meetings.
