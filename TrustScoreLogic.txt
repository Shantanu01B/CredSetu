# CredSetu Trust Score & SHG Score Logic Detail

This document outlines the exact logic used to calculate the Member Trust Score, the Self Help Group (SHG) Health Score, and the conditions under which different statuses and risk alerts are triggered and displayed on the dashboard.

---

## 1. Member Trust Score

The Member Trust Score determines the reliability of an individual member based on their financial and community actions.

- **Base Score:** Every user starts with 100 points.
- **Score Limits (Clamping):** The score cannot go below 0 or above 900.

### Formula
```text
Trust Score = Base Score (100) 
              + (Total Saving Transactions * 2) 
              + (On-Time Repayments * 5) 
              - (Late Repayments * 10) 
              + (Completed Loans * 20) 
              - (Defaulted Loans * 20) 
              + (Meetings Attended * 1) 
              - (Consecutive Misses Penalty: 5 points for every block of 3 misses)

Final Trust Score = MAX(0, MIN(900, Trust Score))
```

### How Does the Score Increase or Decrease?

The score is automatically recalculated based on three main activities:

**A. Transactions (Savings & Repayments)**
- **Saving Deposit:** Every verified saving transaction adds **+2 points**.
- **On-Time Repayment:** Every verified on-time loan repayment adds **+5 points**.
- **Late Repayment:** Every late loan repayment deducts **-10 points**.

**B. Loans**
- **Completed Loan:** Successfully fully repaying a loan adds **+20 points**.
- **Defaulted Loan:** Defaulting on a loan deducts **-20 points**.

**C. Attendance (Community Engagement)**
- **Present:** Attending a meeting adds **+1 point**.
- **Consecutive Absences:** Missing **3 consecutive meetings** deducts **-5 points**. (This penalty applies every time a member misses 3 in a row).

### Member Trust Score Display Statuses (Front-end Logic)

When displayed in tables or lists (e.g., Admin or Bank Dashboards), the Trust Score maps to the following visual badges:
- **Creditworthy / Excellent (Green Status):** Trust Score is **700 or above**.
- **Moderate (Yellow Status):** Trust Score is **between 500 and 699**.
- **High Risk (Red Status):** Trust Score is **below 500**.

> *Note: On the individual Member Dashboard, the badge displays default "Excellent" for aesthetic viewing, but the raw score is displayed dynamically in the range of 0 to 900.*

## 2. Self Help Group (SHG) Health Score

The SHG Health Score measures the overall financial and operational stability of a group as a whole.

- **Range:** The score ranges from 0 to 100.

### Formula
```text
SHG Health Score = Score A + Score B + Score C - Penalty D

Where:
Score A (Avg Trust Score Weight) = MIN((Average Member Trust Score / 9), 100) * 0.40
Score B (Repayment Ratio Weight) = ((Total Repayments - Late Repayments) / Total Repayments * 100) * 0.30
Score C (Fund Growth Weight)     = MIN((Group Total Fund / 10000), 1) * 100 * 0.20
Penalty D (Pending Loan Ratio)   = (Pending Loans / Total Loans * 100) * 0.10

Final SHG Score = ROUND(MAX(0, MIN(100, SHG Health Score)))
```

### How is the SHG Score Calculated?

It consists of 4 weighted components (A, B, C, D) which are calculated and added together (or subtracted!).

**A. Average Member Trust Score (Weight: 40%)**
- The system takes the average Trust Score of all members in the group.
- It is scaled down from 900 to 100, and then multiplied by 0.40.
- *Example: If average trust score is 900, this component gives 40 points.*

**B. Loan Repayment Ratio (Weight: 30%)**
- The ratio of on-time loan repayments to total expected repayments for the group. 
- *Example: 100% on-time repayment history gives the full 30 points.*

**C. Fund Growth Rate (Weight: 20%)**
- Evaluated against a target fund goal (Target: ₹10,000).
- If the group has ₹10,000 or more in total funds, they get the full 20 points. If they have ₹5,000, they get 10 points.

**D. Pending Loan Ratio Penalty (Weight: 10% Penalty)**
- The percentage of active loans that are still stuck in the "pending" state.
- *Example: If 50% of the loan pool is unapproved/pending, 5 points are deducted from the total.*

### SHG Health Score Display Statuses

Different dashboards categorize the 0-100 SHG score slightly differently to give Bank Managers or Admins the right perspective:

**Bank Manager Dashboard View:**
- **Good (Green Badge):** Score is **70 or above**.
- **Moderate (Yellow Badge):** Score is **between 50 and 69**.
- **Risk / Poor (Red Badge):** Score is **below 50**.

**Admin Control Panel View:**
- **Excellent (Green Badge):** Score is **80 or above**.
- **Good (Blue Badge):** Score is **between 60 and 79**.
- **Moderate (Yellow Badge):** Score is **between 40 and 59**.
- **Risky (Red Badge):** Score is **below 40**.

## 3. Automated Risk Engine & Alerts

The background Risk Engine continuously monitors the SHG and triggers alerts that are displayed on the Bank Dashboard's "Risk Watchlist".

These are the exact conditions that will trigger a risk alert:

- **Group Level Risk (High Severity / Red):** 
  Triggered if the **SHG Health Score drops below 50**.
  *Message: "Group Health Score is critical (Score)"*

- **Member Level - Trust Score Risk (High Severity / Red):**
  Triggered if any **Member's Trust Score drops below 60**. 
  *Message: "Member Name: Trust Score is critically low (Score)"*

- **Member Level - Repayment Risk (Medium Severity / Orange or Yellow):**
  Triggered if any single member accumulates **2 or more late transactions**.
  *Message: "Member Name: Has X late repayments"*

- **Member Level - Engagement Risk (Low/Medium Severity / Yellow):**
  Triggered if any member **misses the last 3 consecutive meetings**.
  *Message: "Member Name: Missed last 3 meetings"*
